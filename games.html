Games

Current project: GoFast

Currently, I'm working on 2D Platformer focused on multiplayer speedrunning. It's called GoFast and it's really simple, you just need to get to the end as fast as you can. There are many enemies in the way to stop your progress but you can try to kill them and collect as many coins as you can to get a bigger score. There's two types of scores, the normal one that is dependent on your killed enemies, collected coins and time finished under the recommended time.

Controls

Keyboard

A/D or Left/Right arrows: Move left and right
Spacebar: Jump or AirJump
Z or K: Hold to Dash
X or L: Attack
Escape: Quit game (Desktop version only)

Controller

Left joystick or Arrow keys: Move left and right
Bottom button: Jump or AirJump
Right trigger: Hold to Dash
Right button: Attack


As this is an early build, some issues are to be expected. If you notice anything and wish to let me know, you can always contact me at beaniebytedev@gmail.com. Any feedback is greatly appreciated!

There are currently no menus (they'll be coming very soon), so to pause the game just click the game window and then outside of it. Instead of playing in the browser, you can download the game here. To play it, unzip the folder and execute the file GoFast.
